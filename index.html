Welcome to ASME @ Illinois!

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide all students of the
Department of Mechanical Science and
Engineering with an open-membership
society that creates a dynamic bridge
between students, faculty, and corporations.
We host events and programs that are
catered to enhancing the experience of
engineers and fostering a community among
students at the University of Illinois at
Urbana-Champaign.
